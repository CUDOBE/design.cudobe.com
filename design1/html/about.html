About Codobe

Building trust in global trade — one verified document, one secure payment at a time.

Our Mission

Codobe was born from a simple yet powerful observation: cross-border trade still suffers from trust gaps, document friction, and payment risks — especially when dealing with emerging markets.

We exist to remove these barriers so that small and medium-sized exporters and importers can trade confidently — anywhere in the world.

Trust First

Every transaction is built on verified documents and conditional escrow — not blind faith.

Speed Without Risk

Faster documentation, real-time compliance checks, instant verification — trade moves at modern speed.

Borderless by Design

Built for emerging markets, stablecoin-ready, and compliant across jurisdictions.
