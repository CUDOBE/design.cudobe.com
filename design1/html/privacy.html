Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Codobe ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website codobe.com, use our platform, or interact with our services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

Important note: Codobe processes trade-related documents that may contain personal data, business information, and sensitive commercial details. We treat all uploaded content with the highest level of care and security.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, company/business name
  • Role selection: Importer, Exporter, Importer & Exporter, Freelance CHA Agent
  • Uploaded documents: invoices, bills of lading, certificates of origin, packing lists, etc. (may contain names, addresses, tax IDs, signatures, etc.)
  • Communication data: messages sent to support, feedback, or chat

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type/version, operating system, device type
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, features used, verification requests
  • Cookies and similar technologies: session management, analytics (Google Analytics), security (reCAPTCHA)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • reCAPTCHA / Google: risk scores to detect bots and abuse
  • Compliance databases: sanctions lists, restricted party screening (via third-party APIs)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service (document verification, compliance checks)
  • To authenticate users and prevent fraud/abuse
  • To communicate with you (account notifications, support responses)
  • To comply with legal obligations (KYC/AML requirements, sanctions screening, tax/reporting laws)
  • To analyze usage trends and enhance platform performance
  • To protect the security and integrity of the Service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in these limited cases:

  • With service providers: cloud hosting (AWS/GCP), OCR/AI providers, compliance screening partners (under strict data processing agreements)
  • For legal reasons: to comply with laws, respond to subpoenas, protect rights/safety
  • In business transfers: during merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • With your consent: if you explicitly request sharing (e.g., sharing verification report with counterparty)

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active + 2 years after closure (for audit/legal purposes)
  • Uploaded documents: retained for 12–36 months depending on verification type and legal requirements (you can request deletion earlier)
  • Logs/analytics: anonymized usage data retained up to 26 months

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure document processing (temporary storage, automatic deletion after use where possible)

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is consent-based)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g., in India: Data Protection Board)

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@codobe.com.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside India (e.g., USA, EU, Singapore) by our service providers. We use appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions) to protect cross-border transfers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes will be notified via email or in-app notice.
